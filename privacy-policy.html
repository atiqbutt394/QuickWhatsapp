Privacy Policy

App Name: QuickWhatsApp
Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how QuickWhatsApp ("the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Recent phone numbers – numbers you have opened a chat with, saved to speed up future use.
  • Message templates – custom message templates you create within the App.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location.
  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting tools.
  • We do not show advertisements.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or any other device sensor.
  • The App does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Data stored locally (recent numbers and message templates) is used solely to improve your experience within the App — for example, showing your recent contacts so you can quickly open a chat again.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any data to third parties. All data remains on your device.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual recent numbers or templates from within the App.
  • Clearing the App's data via Android Settings → Apps → QuickWhatsApp → Clear Data.
  • Uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data.

6. Third-Party Services

The App launches WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business when you open a chat. Please refer to WhatsApp's own privacy policy for how they handle your data once you are redirected to their app.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
